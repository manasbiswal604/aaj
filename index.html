Welcome to Our Journal

Our Demo Journal is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal that publishes original research articles, review articles, and short communications in various fields of science and technology. Our mission is to provide a platform for researchers and practitioners to share their knowledge and findings, fostering innovation and advancements in the field.

Frequency : Bi-Monthly (6 issue per year)

Topics Covered: Various scientific and engineering fields

Accepted Language: English Only

Submit Online: Click Here

Type of Articles: Papers should be result-oriented and plagiarism-free (less than 10% including reference section). The paper title should not use generic terms like ‘Overview, Brief, Review, Case study, Study, Survey/ Approach, Comparative, Investigation, comparison’.

Please ensure that any submitted manuscript has not been previously submitted or published elsewhere. We strictly advise submitting only original and plagiarism-free articles for consideration; otherwise, they will be rejected without a response.

All received manuscripts undergo a double-blind peer review process, and the final decision is based on high levels of quality, originality, and significant contribution to existing knowledge. It is crucial that once your article is submitted to our journal, it cannot be submitted or presented anywhere else unless it is officially rejected. This paper will not be withdrawn or presentable in any other journal, conference, magazine, or media without the explicit permission of our journal.

Publication Charges of Our Journal

If an article is accepted for publication, authors are required to pay a nominal processing charge. This fee covers the online publication of the entire research paper, assignment of an individual Digital Object Identifier (DOI) number to each article, individual e-Certificates for all authors, indexing processes, editorial fees, website maintenance, and journal infrastructure costs.

Abstracting, Indexing and Archiving

Our journal is indexed and archived by various reputable services, including CrossRef, DOI, Google Scholar, EuroPub, Index Copernicus, Internet Archive, Indian Citation Index (ICI), Academia, Zenodo, Mendeley, Semantic Scholar, Scope Database, ResearchBib, CiteFactor, Follow Science, DRJI, J-Gate, ScienceGate, AcademicKeys, and more.
