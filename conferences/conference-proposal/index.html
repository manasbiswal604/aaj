Conference Proposal

Embrace innovation and collaboration by partnering with the International Journal of Advanced Research and Interdisciplinary Scientific Endeavors (IJARISE) for your next conference. Whether virtual or offline, IJARISE offers a sustainable platform to publish scientific papers and exchange ideas, ensuring your conference leaves a lasting impact.

Types of Conferences:

  1. Annual Conferences: Set the stage for annual gatherings of thought leaders and researchers.
  2. Quarterly Conferences: Stay ahead of the curve with regular updates and discussions.
  3. Technical and Non-Technical Conferences: Cater to diverse audiences with specialized and general-interest events.
  4. Student Conferences: Empower the next generation of scholars with dedicated student-focused conferences.
  5. Event Summaries and Reports: Document and disseminate key insights and outcomes from your events.

Publication Offers:

Collaborating with IJARISE opens doors to prime publishing opportunities. Conference papers and proceedings will be showcased in our journal, with the added prestige of being submitted for Scopus, Web of Science, and Ei-Compendex Indexing.

About IJARISE:

International Journal of Advanced Research and Interdisciplinary Scientific Endeavours stands at the forefront of multidisciplinary research, providing a dynamic platform for cutting-edge scientific papers and advanced research. Unlike conventional publishers, we embrace diverse contributions, from concepts and hypotheses to early research works and negative results, fostering inclusive scientific discourse.

Key Objectives:

  • Serve as a premier forum for groundbreaking research across multidisciplinary disciplines.
  • Promote inclusivity by accepting technical papers from students and academic professionals.
  • Foster innovation by embracing diverse scholarly contributions.
  • Bridge the scientific gap between current and exemplary research.
  • Uphold rigorous editorial standards and peer review processes.
  • Actively engage with the scientific community to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange worldwide.

IJARISE Focus and Scope:

Our journal is dedicated to publishing groundbreaking scientific papers across various disciplines. We invite researchers to join us in revolutionizing modern research and driving innovation. Let's collaborate to make a difference in reporting advanced scientific articles.

For inquiries and collaboration opportunities, contact us at contact@ijarise.org and embark on a journey of discovery with IJARISE Journal.
