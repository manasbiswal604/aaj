Journal Management

IJARISE uses Open Journal Systems (OJS) which was developed as part of the Public Knowledge Project (PKP) revolutionizes the management and publication of scholarly journals and their scientific articles, offering comprehensive support throughout the entire publishing process. From manuscript submissions to online publication and indexing, OJS streamlines workflows enhances research visibility and promotes open access principles.

Key Features of OJS:

  1. Refereed Publishing Process: OJS facilitates rigorous peer review, ensuring the quality and integrity of published research. With its robust management systems, OJS empowers editors and reviewers to maintain high scholarly standards.
  2. Indexing and Visibility: OJS provides finely-grained indexing of research articles, enhancing their discoverability and impact. By adhering to open-access principles, journals using OJS contribute to the global dissemination of knowledge and promote public access to scholarly research.
  3. DOI Creation and Management: OJS automates the creation and submission of Digital Object Identifiers (DOIs), providing unique identifiers for published articles. Through integration with DOI Foundation and Crossref, OJS ensures the persistent and reliable citation of scholarly content.
  4. Review Management: OJS streamlines the review process, allowing editors to efficiently manage peer reviews and make informed decisions on manuscript submissions. With features for tracking reviewer comments and revisions, OJS supports transparent and fair evaluation of research.
  5. Publishing Support: From manuscript submission to online publication, OJS offers seamless workflows for authors, editors, and publishers. With user-friendly interfaces and intuitive tools, OJS simplifies the publication process while maintaining a high level of professionalism.

Journal Hosting with In-motion Hosting:

In-motion Hosting provides reliable hosting services for OJS journals, ensuring secure and stable access to published content. With robust infrastructure and dedicated support, In-motion Hosting offers an ideal platform for hosting scholarly journals and managing online publications.

DOI Management with DOI Foundation and Crossref:

OJS, a journal management system, can integrate with DOI Foundation and Crossref to manage Digital Object Identifiers (DOIs) for published articles. By assigning DOIs, journals can enhance the citability and accessibility of research and journal articles, which helps in accurate attribution and long-term preservation of scholarly content.

OJS provides a comprehensive solution for journal management, publication, and indexing. With its user-friendly interface and powerful features, OJS helps scholarly publishers increase the quality, visibility, and impact of their journals in the global research community.
