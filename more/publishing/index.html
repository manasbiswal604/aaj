Publishing at IJARISE

Publishing scientific papers in IJARISE offers a multitude of advantages, ensuring that researchers and scholars receive the recognition and support they deserve in the academic community comparative to papers published in scopus indexed journals and UGC care list journals. Here are the key benefits:

  1. Quality Review: Each submission undergoes a rigorous peer-review process, ensuring high-quality standards and providing valuable feedback for authors to enhance their work.
  2. National and International Recognition: IJARISE publishes and helps to get recognition similar to scopus indexed journals and UGC CARE list journals, granting national and international recognition to published papers.
  3. Global Outreach via Major Journal Indexing Databases: By being indexed in major databases, papers published in IJARISE reach a global audience of researchers and scholars, fostering collaboration and dissemination of knowledge.
  4. DOI for Each Published Content: Every published paper receives a Digital Object Identifier (DOI), ensuring persistent and reliable identification for citation and reference purposes.
  5. Diverse Publication Opportunities: IJARISE accepts a wide range of content types including (but not limited to) early career papers, ideations, concepts, negative results, hypothesis, thesis, conference papers, conference proceedings, feedbacks, news, short abstracts, datasets, perspectives, and student papers.
  6. Article Rewriting Support: Authors receive assistance in refining and improving their articles to meet the journal's standards, enhancing the clarity and impact of their research.
  7. Technical Feedback for Future Advancements: Authors benefit from technical feedback provided by experts in their respective fields, aiding in the advancement of their research and future projects.
  8. Content Registration and Long-Term Preservation: All published content is registered via CrossRef and DOI Foundation, ensuring long-term online preservation for continued access and reference.
  9. Low-Cost Publication and Fully Open-Access: IJARISE offers affordable publication fees and operates on a fully open-access model, making research findings freely accessible to readers worldwide.
  10. Publication Certificates and Multidisciplinary Subjects: Authors can request publication certificates, and IJARISE welcomes submissions from multidisciplinary fields, facilitating collaboration and multidisciplinary research.

Publishing scientific papers in IJARISE not only ensures the dissemination of valuable research but also provides authors with the recognition, support, and opportunities necessary for their academic and professional growth.
