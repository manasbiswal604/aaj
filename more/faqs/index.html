FAQ's

IJARISE stands for International Journal of Advanced Research and Interdisciplinary Scientific Endeavours. It is a scholarly journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research articles across various disciplines, including scientific papers and research papers.

Yes, IJARISE follows a rigorous peer-review process to ensure the quality and validity of published articles. All submitted manuscripts undergo a thorough evaluation by experts in their respective fields. The quality of papers published here is comparable to papers published in scopus indexed journals and UGC CARE list journals.

IJARISE publishes original research articles, review papers, case studies, and short communications covering a wide range of multidisciplinary scientific topics.

Manuscripts can be submitted online through the journal's website using the "Submit Manuscript" option. Authors are required to adhere to the submission guidelines provided on the website.

The publication charges vary based on the category of the author (student, research scholar, professional). Detailed information on publication fees is available on the journal's website (APC). Fee waiver options for free publication are also available for authors facing financial constraints.

The peer review process typically takes 2-3 weeks. However, the duration may vary depending on factors such as the complexity of the manuscript and the availability of reviewers.

Manuscripts are evaluated based on originality, significance of findings, methodological rigor, clarity of presentation, and adherence to ethical guidelines.

Those interested in becoming reviewers can submit their CV and a brief statement of expertise to the editorial office at editor@ijarise.org. Reviewer appointments are made based on qualifications and expertise.

Yes, authors may request article retractions under certain circumstances such as errors in data or ethical concerns. Retraction requests are evaluated by the editorial office.

You can reach the editorial office of IJARISE by emailing contact@ijarise.org. Our team is available to assist with any inquiries or concerns.

Yes, publication certificates are available upon request. Authors can contact the editorial office to obtain their publication certificates.

Yes, authors are required to follow the formatting guidelines provided on the journal's website to ensure consistency and clarity in manuscript presentation.

You can subscribe to the journal's newsletter or follow us on social media platforms to receive updates on new publications, calls for papers, and other announcements. Additionally, regular visits to the journal's website will provide access to the latest content and news.
